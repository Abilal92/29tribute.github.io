Khabib Abdulmanapovich Nurmagomedov

born 20 September 1988 is a Russian former professional mixed martial artist.

2020

Khabib vs Gaethje

Gaethje was roped into the fray on short notice and never stood a chance against Khabib who, unbeknownst to most, was planning on retiring after the fight.

2019

Khabib vs Dustin Poirier

There were few occasions over the course of his career where Khabib looked to be in a spot of bother. However, Dustin Poirier did come agonisingly close to submitting the Russian to snap his proud unbeaten record.

2018

Khabib vs McGregor

There was only ever going to be one winner here. Khabib's fight with bitter rival McGregor is the highest-grossing pay-per-view in the history of the UFC.

2018

Khabib vs Al laquinta

The fight went the distance with Al laquinta more than holding his own. However, the scorecards would reveal that Khabib had won all five rounds, showing just how mentally tough he was following a seven-day period that could have completely derailed his preparations for the fight.

2017

Khabib vs Edson Barboza

At the time, Khabib labelled this particular fight his 'most dominance performance yet'. Khabib overwhelmed Barboza, executing every aspect of his game to perfection to make the judges job an easy one.

2016

Khabib vs Darell Horcher

Florida was the stage for yet another show of sheer lightweight domination from 'The Eagle'. Horcher simply couldn't handle the physical speed and prowess of Khabib, with the fight ending in a TKO.

2016

Khabib vs Michael Johnson

Number one rule when fighting Khabib? Don't let it go to ground. Michael Johnson learned that lesson the hard way after a promising start against Nurmagomedov.
